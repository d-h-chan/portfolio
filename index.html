About Me

Hello again! My name is Daniel Chan. I live in the San Francisco Bay Area and went to college at UC San Diego. Growing up, I found myself drawn to the sciences, like biology and physics, as well as the more right-brained hobby of making music. This resulted in my pursuit of web development as a career, where I get to exercise both the problem-solving and creative parts of my mind.

When I'm not working, you can find me at the gym making gains, working on my Youtube gaming channel, or playing piano or guitar. I'm always looking for new opportunities to work with other developers, so if you'd like to get in touch send me an email!
